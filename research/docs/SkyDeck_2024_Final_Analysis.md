## 🎓 **Methodology Lessons**

### **What Worked ✅**
- Web research with Google search
- Looking for "ML", "LLM", "Deep Learning" in descriptions
- Checking company websites and tech news

### **What Didn't Work ❌**
- Name-only detection
- Relying on accelerator's own data
- Simple keyword matching without context

### **Best Practice for Future Analysis**
1. **Don't trust name-only classification**
2. **Research a sample (10-20 companies)**
3. **Extrapolate conservatively**
4. **Document uncertainty ranges**
